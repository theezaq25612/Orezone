The Foundation

A Legacy of Gold Excellence

Kinetix Orezone SMC Limited was established in Uganda with a clear mandate: to provide East Africa with access to verified, investment-grade gold products through a process built on integrity and professional care.

Our founders recognized that the gold market in the region lacked a trusted, transparent operator. That gap became our purpose. We built Kinetix Orezone from the ground up — sourcing from certified producers, establishing rigorous authentication protocols, and creating a client experience worthy of the precious metal we trade.

Today, we serve individual investors, institutional clients, collectors, and those looking for bespoke luxury jewelry — all with the same commitment to quality that defined our founding principles.

Our Commitment

Trust & Assurance

Every transaction, every product, every interaction is built on the foundation of verifiable trust.

01

Authenticity Verification

Each gold product in our inventory undergoes a multi-stage authentication process. We use XRF (X-ray fluorescence) spectroscopy and fire assay testing to confirm purity levels before any product enters our collection. All bars and coins carry hallmarks from accredited mints and assay offices. Verification documentation is available upon request for every purchase.

02

Secure Transactions

Financial security is paramount in precious metal trading. All transactions are conducted through verified banking channels and formal financial instruments. We maintain strict KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) compliance. Client financial data is encrypted, never shared with third parties, and handled with complete discretion at all times.

03

Quality Assurance Process

Our quality assurance is a structured, multi-checkpoint process. Incoming gold is weighed on calibrated balances, tested for purity, examined for condition, and compared against international standards. Products are packaged in tamper-evident, certified enclosures. Each lot is documented with a unique identifier traceable through our full audit chain.

04

Client Privacy & Confidentiality

We understand that wealth is personal. Client identities, transaction volumes, and purchase histories are handled with absolute discretion. We do not share, sell, or disclose client information under any circumstances. Our internal data protocols are designed to protect high-net-worth individuals who require the highest level of privacy in their gold dealings.

Our Gold Guarantee

If any product we supply does not meet the certified purity and weight specifications stated at point of sale, we will provide a full replacement or complete refund — no questions asked. This is our unwavering commitment to every client.
