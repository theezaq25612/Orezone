Testing & Evaluation

Precision Assessment You Can Rely On

Our multi-stage evaluation process eliminates uncertainty and guarantees complete accuracy.

01
XRF Spectrometry Analysis

Non-destructive X-ray fluorescence scanning provides instant elemental composition data across all surface areas of your gold.

02
Fire Assay (Where Required)

For high-volume or complex samples, our licensed assay process conducts traditional cupellation for definitive purity results.

03
Precision Weighing

Calibrated to ±0.01g, our certified balances are regularly verified against national measurement standards.

04
Live Market-Rate Offer

Offers are generated in real-time referencing LBMA spot prices — transparent arithmetic, no hidden deductions.

Our Commitments

Six Pillars of Assurance

Every pillar is operationally implemented — not aspirational. Verification available upon request at our Kampala premises.

01

Scientific Authenticity Testing

Every gold submission undergoes multi-method scientific testing. XRF spectrometry, acid testing, and fire assay are applied as appropriate to deliver definitive purity results. Test equipment is calibrated to international standards and documentation is available for review.

02

Confidential Transactions

All client information is protected under our formal privacy policy and staff NDA agreements. Your transaction details are never disclosed to third parties without explicit written consent. Data is stored on encrypted, access-controlled servers.

03

Live Market Pricing

Our offers are benchmarked against real-time LBMA spot rates, visible to you during the evaluation. No artificial markdowns, no opaque calculations — just transparent arithmetic tied to the same rates used by international bullion banks.

04

Documented Procedures

Every transaction generates a complete paper trail: weight certificates, purity reports, offer documents, and settlement confirmations — all provided to you in writing. Nothing is verbal only; every commitment is formalised.

05

Regulatory Compliance

Kinetix Orezone SMC Limited operates under full regulatory compliance, including anti-money laundering protocols, KYC verification, and all applicable precious metals trading regulations in Uganda and East Africa.

06

Guaranteed Settlement

Once an offer is accepted, payment is guaranteed and executed immediately. We maintain sufficient liquidity reserves to honour every transaction without delay, regardless of size.
